**Finding 1: Humalog Timing Protocols**
- **Compound**: Insulin lispro (Humalog)
- **Dosage**: 5-10 IU pre-workout
- **Timing**: 15 minutes before resistance training
- **Mechanism**: Rapid-acting insulin creates anabolic environment by increasing muscle glycogen storage and amino acid uptake
- **Evidence Quality**: Anecdotal reports from bodybuilding forums, not peer-reviewed
- **Cross-Domain Connections**: Synergizes with branched-chain amino acids (BCAAs) taken concurrently

**Finding 2: Intra-Workout Insulin**
- **Compound**: Humalog
- **Dosage**: 2-5 IU during workout
- **Timing**: Midway through 60-minute resistance session
- **Mechanism**: Maintains insulin spike during catabolic stress phase
- **Evidence Quality**: Case report from competitive bodybuilder
- **Cross-Domain Interactions**: Potential hypoglycemia risk when combined with beta-blockers

**Finding 3: Post-Workout Insulin**
- **Compound**: Humalog
- **Dosage**: 3-7 IU immediately post-workout
- **Timing**: Within 10 minutes of workout completion
- **Mechanism**: Enhances glycogen replenishment and protein synthesis
- **Evidence Quality**: Anecdotal evidence from bodybuilding community
- **Cross-Domain Effects**: May interact with creatine monohydrate absorption

**Finding 4: Starting Dosages**
- **Compound**: Humalog
- **Dosage**: 0.5 IU per 10g carbohydrates
- **Timing**: Meal-dependent
- **Mechanism**: Baseline glycemic control
- **Evidence Quality**: Clinical diabetes guidelines repurposed for bodybuilding
- **Cross-Domain Note**: Requires monitoring when combined with thyroid hormones

**Finding 5: Glucose Thresholds**
- **Compound**: Glucose monitoring
- **Thresholds**: <70 mg/dL requires intervention
- **Timing**: Every 30 minutes during protocol
- **Mechanism**: Prevents hypoglycemia
- **Evidence Quality**: Standard medical protocol adapted
- **Cross-Domain Connection**: Metformin may lower thresholds

**Finding 6: GH-Insulin Synergy**
- **Compound**: Recombinant GH
- **Dosage**: 2-4 IU daily
- **Timing**: Injected 2 hours post-insulin
- **Mechanism**: Complementary anabolic effects and nutrient partitioning
- **Evidence Quality**: Anecdotal bodybuilder logs
- **Cross-Domain Effects**: Risk of fluid retention with concurrent AAS use

**Finding 7: IGF-1 LR3 Timing**
- **Compound**: IGF-1 LR3
- **Dosage**: 20-40 mcg
- **Timing**: Injected 30 minutes after insulin
- **Mechanism**: Extended half-life allows sustained anabolic signaling
- **Evidence Quality**: Case study reports
- **Cross-Domain Connection**: May amplify insulin hypoglycemia risk

**Finding 8: Metformin Dosing**
- **Compound**: Metformin
- **Dosage**: 500-1000 mg twice daily
- **Timing**: Morning and evening, separate from insulin
- **Mechanism**: Improves insulin sensitivity
- **Evidence Quality**: Clinical diabetes studies repurposed
- **Cross-Domain Note**: Contraindicated with high-dose NSAIDs

**Finding 9: Carbohydrate Ratios**
- **Specifics**: 1 IU insulin per 8-12g carbohydrates
- **Timing**: Meal-dependent
- **Mechanism**: Optimizes glycogen storage
- **Evidence Quality**: Diabetes nutrition guidelines adapted
- **Cross-Domain Note**: High-GI carbs require tighter monitoring

**Finding 10: Testosterone Interactions**
- **Compound**: Testosterone cypionate
- **Dosage**: 200-600 mg/week
- **Mechanism**: Synergistic muscle growth but complicates glucose management
- **Evidence Quality**: Observational bodybuilder studies
- **Cross-Domain Connection**: Aromatase inhibitors may exacerbate insulin resistance

**Finding 11: Emergency Protocols**
- **Compound**: Glucagon kit
- **Dosage**: 1 mg IM injection
- **Timing**: When glucose <50 mg/dL unresponsive to oral glucose
- **Mechanism**: Catalyzes glycogen breakdown
- **Evidence Quality**: Standard medical emergency protocol
- **Cross-Domain Connection**: Beta-blockers inhibit glucagon efficacy

**Finding 12: Off-Season Insulin Use**
- **Pattern**: 12-week on, 4-week off cycles
- **Dosage**: Tapering by 2 IU/week
- **Mechanism**: Prevents receptor downregulation
- **Evidence Quality**: Anecdotal periodization strategies
- **Cross-Domain Effects**: May need thyroid function monitoring during deload
